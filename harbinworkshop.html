HARBIN SUMMER SCHOOL

In July 2017, the school of architecture of HIT held the Seventh International Joint Workshop on the summer semester.
In the Joint Workshop of this year, Prof. Arno Pronk from TU/e together with Prof. Peng Luo from HIT as the teachers of
this wirkshop, focusing on research and design of special multiple ice structure, launched a course for 3 weeks.

POLYHYDRIC ICE STRUCTURE

Students have to design a mega large polyhydric ice structure. The structure will be made by freezing a cable structure at the inside of an inflatable. After the cable structure can stand on it selve the inflatbable support structure at the outside can be removed en reused for new structures.

From the point of view of the city, a mega ice structure is supposed to be designed and the activities

is divided into three stages: experiment and understanding of technology, application and expression
of design, and synthesis and expansion of knowledge.
